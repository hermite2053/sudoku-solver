# sudoku-solver
Script solving sudoku problem with Python3.

# usage
run `$ python main.py`
and then type problem like below.
Use `0` for the blank cell.

```
1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 9
4 0 6 7 8 9 1 0 3
7 8 9 1 2 3 4 5 6
2 3 4 5 6 7 8 9 1
5 6 7 8 0 1 2 3 4
8 9 1 2 3 4 5 6 7
3 4 5 6 7 8 9 1 2
6 0 8 9 1 2 3 0 5
9 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8
```
